Wake Up With Yolanda!

Finally! My dream has come true! Check out all of my spectacular coffees listed below! There is only one word to describe them and that word is 'delicious'!

My team and I have worked hard to insure that all of my coffees are shade-grown and sourced from a healthy soil-base. No pesticides have been used during the growing process. We've searched out and found only the highest quality beans and pay close and careful attention to the roasting process. The result: well, see and taste for yourself! It's perfection in every cup!

 
 

Yolanda's Signature Blend

Of all the coffees I sampled, this cup of java was my favorite and became the hallmark of my coffee selections! Yolanda's Signature Blend is a fine mixture of beans from Central America, South America, and Africa. With medium body and acidity, my Signature Blend boasts flavors of milk chocolate, carmelized sugar, and orange zest. The result is scrumptuous! Try it for yourself!
